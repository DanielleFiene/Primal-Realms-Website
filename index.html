PRIMALREALMS

One exile. Five eras. A bloodline that outlasts empires.

You wake up alone. The fire is dying. Something howls in the dark.

Generations later, your descendants rule the same ground where you once fought to survive.

Between those moments lies a living world of mammoths, saber-toothed predators, cave bears, shifting seasons, and a settlement you shape with every hard-won choice.

The Heart of Primal Realms

  • ▪ The Primal Wild — Track mammoths through the valley, read blood trails in the rain, trust your companions, and learn when predators like cave bears and saber-toothed hunters are warning you to turn back.
  • ▪ Shape the Land — Build a sanctuary that grows from a temporary camp into a lasting stronghold.
  • ▪ Dynasty & Succession — Death is certain; your bloodline is not. Choose an heir and carry forward skills, alliances, and reputation.
  • ▪ An Unfolding Timeline — Launch into the first two eras, with future epochs waiting to be forged.
  • ▪ Ancient Echoes — Three factions remember how your ancestors treated them, and the world responds accordingly.
