PRIMALREALMS

One exile. Five eras. A bloodline that outlasts empires.

You wake up alone. The fire is dying. Something howls in the dark.

Generations later, your bloodline rules from the same ground where you almost froze to death.

The world between those two moments is yours to shape.

The Heart of Primal Realms

  • ▪ The Primal Wild — The world is a living, breathing ecosystem. Trust the instincts of your companions—your dog scents what remains unseen as blood trails wash away in the rain. Track, stalk, and earn the allegiance of ancient creatures to turn the wild to your advantage.
  • ▪ Shape the Land — Build your sanctuary from the ground up. Watch your settlement evolve from a temporary mammoth-bone camp into a walled stronghold.
  • ▪ Dynasty & Succession — Mortality is certain; your bloodline is not. When your character's time ends, choose which heir carries the flame. They inherit the skills, alliances, and the reputation of both their parents.
  • ▪ The Unfolding Timeline — Shape history across time. The game launches with the first two eras of content, with more historical epochs waiting to unfold in future updates. Your choices today lay the groundwork for tomorrow.
  • ▪ Ancient Echoes — Forge your path between three distinct factions. Each possesses unique architecture, different values, and a long memory of how your ancestors treated them.
